# ChiefIQ - 3-Minute Hackathon Demo Script

**Speaker (PM):**
"Hi everyone, we are team ChiefIQ.
Today, modern executives waste up to 20% of their week just piecing together fragmented context across emails, Teams, Planner, and SharePoint. They are constantly reacting to fires instead of preventing them.

Meet **ChiefIQ**—your AI Chief of Staff for Microsoft 365. 

*(Switch to Dashboard View)*
Unlike standard Copilots that wait for you to ask a question, ChiefIQ is proactive. Look at our Executive Dashboard. ChiefIQ continuously analyzes organizational signals in the background using a 7-agent LangGraph architecture. It calculates our Org Health Score, detects anomalies, and surfaces the top AI recommendations ranked by urgency and impact.
Notice this red alert: It identified a critical vendor delivery risk on Project Phoenix before anyone even reported it.

*(Switch to Risk Intelligence View)*
If we dive into the Risk Intelligence center, we don't just see a dashboard. We see the AI's reasoning. ChiefIQ extracted the root cause from a missed email thread and a Teams standup transcript, determined the velocity is 'Worsening', and automatically generated an escalation path to the VP of Operations.

*(Switch to Ask ChiefIQ Chat)*
Let's see the conversational power. I'll ask: 'What are the biggest risks threatening Project Phoenix?'
*(Type query into Chat)*
Notice the Agent Reasoning Trace on the right. This isn't a single LLM call. Our Knowledge Retrieval agent fetches the data, the Context Synthesis agent builds a graph, and our Risk Intelligence agent analyzes it. 
Every claim is backed by a verifiable citation card—linking directly to the source email or Teams message. We call this our 'Explainability Trace', ensuring executives can trust the AI.

*(Switch to Decision Replay View)*
Finally, our flagship enterprise feature: The Decision Replay Engine. 
Ever wonder why a decision was made 6 months ago? Select 'Cloud Migration Vendor Approval'. ChiefIQ reconstructs the exact timeline of emails, meetings, and approvals that led to the outcome, providing an instant audit trail.

**Conclusion:**
ChiefIQ integrates deeply with the Microsoft Graph API to transform raw M365 data into proactive executive intelligence. It's built on a scalable Azure-native architecture and is ready to deploy to any enterprise tenant today. 
Thank you."
